Introduction and Executive Summary

Men’s Health Institute (MHI) is a new men’s health clinic in Albufeira, Portugal, led by Dr. Rui Graça, an internationally credentialed urologist, and supported by his wife, Cecilia.

This 9-month marketing strategy outlines a phased approach to achieve fast yet sustainable patient growth under a low-cash, high-effort model.

The plan leverages MHI’s key strengths – from its English-speaking expertise and central Algarve location to its differentiated services (TRT, ED treatments, holistic wellness) and AI- powered personalized treatment plans – to build a strong local foundation and progressively expand its reach.

The strategy is structured in three 3-month phases:

  • Phase 1 (Months 1–3): Establish the foundation and create local buzz in Albufeira, attracting nearby residents and expats through organic efforts.
  • Phase 2 (Months 4–6): Scale up during the tourist high season with modest paid ads and partnerships, capturing visitors and increasing brand authority.
  • Phase 3 (Months 7–9): Expand online outreach across Portugal and EU English- speaking markets, scaling successful tactics and enhancing patient retention via technology.

Throughout all phases, we focus on low-cost, high-impact tactics – e.g. SEO, content marketing, community engagement, and referrals – while minimizing ad spend initially (small targeted Google Ads and retargeting) and increasing budget only as ROI is proven.

Key performance indicators (KPIs) will be tracked closely (inquiries, conversion rates, SEO rankings, patient retention, engagement) to measure success and guide adjustments.

By month 9, MHI should have a robust local patient base, strong online presence, and momentum to continue growing both locally and internationally, all while maintaining its premium yet approachable identity.
